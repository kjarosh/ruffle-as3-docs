Dil Sürümü: ActionScript 3.0

Çalışma Zamanı Sürümleri: Flash Player 9, Flash Player 10, AIR (unsupported)

If the value is true, the reviewer is optional. If the value is false, the reviewer is mandatory. A review stage is marked complete if all mandatory participants have completed their tasks when the stage duration expires. If there are mandatory participants who have not completed their tasks when the stage duration expires, the stage is marked expired.

Varsayılan değer şudur false.
