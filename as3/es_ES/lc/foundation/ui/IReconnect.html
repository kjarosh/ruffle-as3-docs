Paquetelc.foundation.ui
Interfazpublic interface IReconnect extends IUIComponent , IFlexDisplayObject , IBitmapDrawable , IEventDispatcher

Versión del lenguaje: ActionScript 3.0
Versión de producto: Adobe Digital Enterprise Platform Document Services - Workspace 9
Versiones de motor de ejecución: AIR (unsupported), Flash Player 9, Flash Player 10

The IReconnect interface is implemented for permitting users to reconnect to Workspace.

All login pages must implement the IReconnect interface.
