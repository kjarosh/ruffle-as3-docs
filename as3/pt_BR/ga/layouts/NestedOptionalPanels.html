Versão de produto: Adobe Digital Enterprise Platform Document Services - Guides 9
Versões de runtime: AIR 1.0, Flash Player 9, Flash Player 10

Defines the default Nested Panels panel layout available in the Guide Design perspective within Adobe Digital Enterprise Platform Document Services - Workbench 10.0.

Nests the selected panel under a radio button or a check box when the Guide filler selects a button or check box. The panel disappears when the button or check box is deselected.

For more information about creating panel layouts, see Customizing Guides Using Flex Builder.
