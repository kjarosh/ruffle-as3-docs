Versão da linguagem: ActionScript 3.0
Versão de produto: OSMF 1.0
Versões de runtime: Flash Player 10, AIR 1.5

F4MLoader is a loader that is capable of loading F4M files. F4M files are XML documents that adhere to the Flash Media Manifest format, and which represent all of the information needed to load and play a media file.
