Classepublic class AttachmentEvent
HerançaAttachmentEvent Event Object

Versão da linguagem: ActionScript 3.0
Versão de produto: Adobe Digital Enterprise Platform Experience Services - Task Management 10
Versões de runtime: AIR 2.6, Flash Player 10.2

The AttachmentEvent event is dispatched when the user adds, removes and uploads attachments. The event is is also dispatched when the upload of an attachment fails.
