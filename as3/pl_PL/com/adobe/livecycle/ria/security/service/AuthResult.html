DziedziczenieAuthResult Object
Implementuje IAuthResult

Wersja języka: ActionScript 3.0
Wersja produktu: Adobe Digital Enterprise Platform Experience Services - Security 10
Wersje środowiska wykonawczego: AIR 2.6, Flash Player 10.2

The AuthResult object is returned as part of calls to perform Single Sign On (SSO) and login methods of ISecurityManager service. Using the status property the further flow can be decided.
