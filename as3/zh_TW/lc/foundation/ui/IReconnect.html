介面public interface IReconnect extends IUIComponent , IFlexDisplayObject , IBitmapDrawable , IEventDispatcher

語言版本: ActionScript 3.0
產品版本: Adobe Digital Enterprise Platform Document Services - Workspace 9
執行階段版本: AIR (unsupported), Flash Player 9, Flash Player 10

The IReconnect interface is implemented for permitting users to reconnect to Workspace.

All login pages must implement the IReconnect interface.
