Classepublic class ImageLoader
EreditarietàImageLoader LoaderBase EventDispatcher Object

Versione linguaggio: ActionScript 3.0
Versione prodotto: OSMF 1.0
Versioni runtime: Flash Player 10, AIR 1.5

ImageLoader is a loader that is capable of loading and displaying image files.

The image is loaded from the URL provided by the resource property of the LoadTrait that is passed to the ImageLoader's load() method.
