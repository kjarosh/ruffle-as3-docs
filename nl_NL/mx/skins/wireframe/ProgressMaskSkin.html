Taalversie: ActionScript 3.0
Productversie: Flex 4
Runtimeversies: Flash Player 10, AIR 1.5

The wireframe skin for the mask of the MX ProgressBar component's determinate and indeterminate bars. The mask defines the area in which the progress bar or indeterminate progress bar is displayed. By default, the mask defines the progress bar to be inset 1 pixel from the track.
